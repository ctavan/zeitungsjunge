Frequently Asked Questions



Which paper should I choose?

FAZ and Zeit are the best looking ones. Everything else is a matter of taste. I recommend to limit yourself to one or two papers. Otherwise your library will look messy very soon and you are going nuts deleting old papers.

What means morning, noon and evening?

Since we can not control exact times of delivery we decided to be a bit fuzzy here. We use European time, i.e. morning is something between 6am and 9am CET.

What is my Kindle e-mail?

You can send personal documents to your Kindle using your Kindle e-mail. To allow me to send anything you need to add zustellung@zeitungsjunge.tk to your approved personal document e-mail list.

Isn't it filling up my storage pretty quick?

One paper is between 1 and 5 MB, so you will be fine for at leat one year. Nevertheless, even though I might reduce this size by removing the images from the subscribtions in the future you might eventually ran into trouble with the space on your device or the 5GB online quota. But you can delete items on your device or online after reading. Here is a post which explains how to do this quickly.

Why did you do it?

To practice setting up a web application. The back-end is written in a language called node.js, the project is hosted by Amazon Web Services and I use SendGrid to send the mails. I also use command-line tools from calibre to download and create the papers. The source code is here.
